## Recording Tips

1. **Practice first** - Do a full run-through without recording
2. **Clear audio** - Close windows, turn off fans, speak clearly
3. **Steady screen** - No shaky mouse movements
4. **Use real data** - Show actual analysis results, not fake data
5. **Keep it moving** - 3 minutes goes fast, don't linger
6. **Show don't tell** - Let the UI speak for itself where possible
